Cooking With Soul

Donna Haraway once said
that we are all fabricated hybrids
of machine and organism; in short, we are all cyborgs.
We depend on technological inventions
and we merge with non human elements.
In this project you can create your own cyborg,
or as we call it a "soul".
What elements will your soul include?
What will your soul teach you about the world we live in?

Cook and discover.
