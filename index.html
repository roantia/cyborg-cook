Donna Haraway once said that we
are all fabricated hybrids of machine
and organism. In short, we are all cyborgs.
We depend on technological inventions
and we merge with non human elements.
In this project you can create your own cyborg,
or as we call it a "soul". What elements
will your soul include? What will your soul
teach you about the world we live in?
